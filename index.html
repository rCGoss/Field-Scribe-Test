Quick Start
Field Scribe in five steps
  1. 1Tap 📁 top-right to create a job. Everything you record is saved to that job automatically on this device.
  2. 2Tap the big mic button and call out a size — e.g. "Shaker, 24 by 36, quantity 4." Style and quantity are optional.
  3. 3The app reads it back and listens for a yes/no. No mic, or it misheard? Edit the fields by hand, or use Manual Entry below the mic instead.
  4. 4Review, edit, or delete any line in your list using the ✎ and 🗑 icons.
  5. 5When you're done, use Export to download a CSV, or Email to send it — set a default recipient under Email Settings so it's one tap.
